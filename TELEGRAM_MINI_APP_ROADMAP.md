# Telegram Mini App Roadmap

## Current Stage

The project should stay bot-first for MVP-DI-1. Telegram polling, scenario selection, length/style selection, knowledge-base retrieval, media generation, and feedback can still fit inside the bot flow.

## When To Move To Mini App

Move to a Mini App when the product has more than 3 content modes and the workflow starts requiring persistent visual management: batch review, saved drafts, media generation history, a feedback dashboard, and multiple author profiles.

Suggested threshold: Mini App becomes worthwhile when Dinara needs to compare many outputs, edit drafts, review KB coverage, manage author voice, or inspect feedback in one place instead of clicking through bot messages.

## Recommended Screens

- Dashboard
- Generate
- Knowledge Base
- Author Voice
- Batch Review
- Feedback
- Settings
- Billing later

## Not In Scope Now

Do not implement the Mini App for MVP-DI-1. Keep this as a transition note and continue validating the bot-first workflow locally.
